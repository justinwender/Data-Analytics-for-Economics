### Objective
To empirically test the **Law of One Price** by using the Big Mac Index as a proxy for Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) across international currencies.

---

### Methodology
- Constructed a country-level dataset using publicly available **2015 Big Mac Index** price data from *The Economist*.
- Converted raw local-currency burger prices into **implied PPP exchange rates** relative to the U.S. dollar.
- Compared implied PPP rates to observed market exchange rates to measure **currency misalignment**.
- Quantified the degree of **overvaluation or undervaluation** for each currency as a percentage deviation from parity.

---
